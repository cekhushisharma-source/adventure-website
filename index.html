Adventure Club

Adventure Sports Club @ IIT Kanpur is the forum for adventure enthusiasts, trekkers, climbers and mountaineers in the student community. We work to develop the spirit of adventure in IITians. Our aims are to make the best of the flat terrain around Kanpur while enjoying exciting outdoor activities. Trekking, rock climbing, mountain cycling, skiing and river rafting are some of our adventures.
