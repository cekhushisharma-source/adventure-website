Awards

Best Outgoing Sportsmen

The Games Council, if they find someone most deserving and outstanding, will propose names for this award. The Games & Sports Committee will decide whether to give or not to give this award in a particular year after giving due consideration to the recommendation of the Games Council and approval of the Games & Sports Committee.

Most Promising Incoming Sportsmen

Outstanding sportsmen from amongst the students in the first year of their program of study will be given this award on the recommendation of the Games Council and approval of the Games & Sports Committee.
